Conclusions¶

Here, I conclude my research analysis and try to answer some of the most common questions related to the TMDB movies data with my best abilities. The major drawback was the amount of misssing and null values which resulted in the fall of volume of the data.
I found relation between the budget and the year of release of the movies and it significantly increased with time.
I also found a relation between the popularity and the amount of revenue generated by the movies and it concluded that more popular movies generated more revenue.
There is also a conclusion that the movies of adventure genre are most popular followed by science fiction, action and so on.
And finally I found that profit earned by movies increased with time.
